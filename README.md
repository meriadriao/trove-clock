## âœ¨ Features
Trove is simple to use and easy to love. Here's what you can do:

- **Track focus sessions:** the timer will start the moment you click on focus. After the set amount of minutes, a break session will begin.
- **Make a to-do list:** the first task on your list will be displayed at the top of the page. Once you're done, the next task will appear on it's place.
- **Watch your collection grow:** as you complete focus sessions, a new plant pot will be added to your shelf!
- **Choose your visual:** choose between light and dark mode. You can also trade the pixel font for a sans-serif font.
